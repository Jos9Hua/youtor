FAQs

General

Youtor is a platform that connects students looking for guidance in a certain subject or topic at UBC with exceptional students who excel in that field and can provide the support to succeed!

Signing up as a student or tutor is easy! Just press the Sign Up button located on the top right window and follow the steps show on the screen!

Youtor is a platform based on you and your needs! After every session both the student and the tutor will recieve an email to rate each other. This rating system keeps the platform growing and helps your peers find the right help.

Students

To apply to be a student, click the sign up button on top of the page, select 'student' as your role and follow the on-screen instructions. Its as easy as that!

Please allow a little time for the confirmation email to reach your inbox. However, if after 2 hours the email is not recieved, please send us a copy of your invoice from your bank to support@youtor.com

Due to the abundance of courses offered at UBC, we cannot guarantee a tutor for all the courses. However be on the lookout as we are actively gaining more tutors for a wide variety of courses!

Since we use Stripe as our payment partner, you can pay using your credit and debit cards.

To make the most out of any tutoring session, we recommend using the message area after booking to inform your tutors in advance in regards to exactly what you would like to gain from the session. This enables them to provide you the knowledge streamlined towards your academic success!

Once a session is completed, the student will recieve an email conforming the completion of the course. The student has 24 hours to respond to the email. Once the lesson has been approved, then only will the payment be released to the tutor.

Please note, if not response is recieved, the system will automatically assume approval.

Tutors

To apply to be a tutor, click the sign up button on top of the page, select 'tutor' as your role and follow the on-screen instructions. All students applying to be tutors must submit their offical academic transcript, achieving a grade above a certain threshold in the designated course, to be deemed fit for tutoring.

Changing your settings is easy! All details can be accessed through the 'User Profile' tab once you are logged in. However, making changes to the courses tutored requires a resubmission and approval of the transcript.

Tutors can indicate their weekly availability through an inbuilt schedule on the website. There is no restriction to how the tutor wants to allocate their time. This schedule will be visible to students at the time of booking.
